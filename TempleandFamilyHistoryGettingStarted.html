Temple and Family History: Getting Started


Welcome to the Sugar Land 2nd Ward resource page for Temple and Family History work.


Below are introductory explanations and references to the various aspects of Temple and Family History work. In order to get started, you will need a login to familysearch.org. If you are a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, your membership credentials are your login, otherwise you will need to create an account.


There are a few related areas to the redemption of our deceased ancestors :


  • Data Digitizing (Indexing, reviewing names, verifying places,etc.)

  • Building and Adding to Your Family Tree

  • Temple Work


More information on these categories are listed below:


Data Digitizing (Indexing, reviewing names, verifying places, etc)

  • Searching records for information related to our deceased ancestors. There are many physical records added daily. To facilitate access to these records by a broader audience, these records must be Indexed so their content becomes searchable.

    Here are resources to provide you an introduction and guidance to getting started with Indexing:
    https://www.familysearch.org/indexing/my-indexing

    In addition to Indexing, there are three other areas of opportunity related to digitizing records:

    • Quick Name Review

    • Full Name Review

    • Verify Places

Some may consider these activities as a type of game. For more information, please see:
https://www.familysearch.org/getinvolved/opportunities
and click on “Get Started” or “Find Places” as desired

In addition, to support the various areas of Family Search, phone apps are available for Android and Apple phones:
https://www.familysearch.org/en/mobile-apps/


Building and Adding to Your Family Tree

  • Linking ourselves to our living and deceased ancestors by identifying and recording a small set of essential information. Finding our deceased ancestors is made easier by Indexing as described above.

    Here are resources to provide you an introduction and guidance to getting started with building your Family Tree:
    https://www.familysearch.org/tree/overview

Temple Work

  • Once we have identified and linked our ancestors, we can perform vicariously the saving and exalting ordinances of: Baptism, Confirmation and gift of the Holy Ghost, Ordination to Priesthood for male ancestors, Endowment, Eternal Marraige and Sealing of children to Parents. This work is performed in the Temple.

    You can identify names to take to the Temple according to the type of work you plan on participating in at the Temple through the “Ordinances Ready” feature of the Family Search - Family Tree phone app as well as on the Family Search website:
    https://www.familysearch.org/en/blog/new-ordinances-ready-feature
    Our Ward holds a monthly Ward Temple trip. We target the 1pm Endowment session on the 2nd Saturday of each month, however we encourage you to attend a time or ordinance that works best for you.

    Here are resources for scheduling a Temple visit to ensure you have a spot in the Endowment session:
    Houston, Texas Temple Reservations

    Youth temple trips occur quarterly where baptisms and confirmations are performed. All members, including new members, are encouraged to attend as often as schedules allow to bring the power of the temple into their lives.
