Fort Bend Interfaith Thanksgiving Service, November 21, 7-8 pm, St. Laurence Catholic Church, 3100 Sweetwater Blvd, Sugar Land. The Fort Bend Interfaith Thanksgiving Service returns as an in-person gathering after two years of celebrating virtually. Representatives from Fort Bend's diverse religious traditions will be presenting readings, prayers, songs and dances on the theme of 'Together in Love and Friendship.' An interfaith youth choral group will be participating as well.
